## Dental Procedures & Pricing

### Major Restorative Procedures

#### Dental Implants (Tooth Replacement)
**What it is:** A permanent tooth replacement using a titanium post surgically placed in the jawbone with a crown attached on top.

**US Average Cost:** $4,000 - $6,000
**Cancun Cost:** $1,200 - $1,800
**Savings:** 70-80%

**Timeline:** 6-8 months total (bone integration takes 3-6 months between visits)
- Visit 1 (7-10 days in Cancun): Implant placement surgery
- Visit 2 (7 days): Crown fabrication and placement (typically 4-6 weeks later)
- Recovery: 2-3 weeks minimal activity, then normal function

**Recovery Details:**
- Days 1-3: Rest, soft food diet, no smoking/alcohol
- Week 1-2: Light activity, avoid strenuous exercise
- Month 1: Most patients back to normal diet
- Month 3-6: Bone fully integrates for optimal strength

**Warranty:** 5 years on implant and crown; free replacement if complications arise

**Success Rate:** 95-98% (comparable to US)

**What affects cost:**
- Bone grafting needed (add $800-2,000)
- Multiple implants (cost per implant decreases)
- Materials (titanium vs ceramic abutment)
- Clinic choice

---

#### Crown (Cap)
**What it is:** A protective cover placed over a damaged or weak tooth.

**Materials:**
- Ceramic (most popular): $400-600 Cancun vs $1,200-1,800 US
- Porcelain-fused-to-metal: $350-500 Cancun vs $900-1,400 US
- Full gold: $500-800 Cancun vs $1,500-2,500 US

**Timeline:** 2-3 visits over 7-10 days
- Visit 1: Tooth preparation, temporary crown placement
- Visit 2: Permanent crown fabrication (typically 1 week later)
- Final fit and adjustment

**Recovery:** Minimal; fully functional immediately

**Warranty:** 5 years

---

#### Bridge (Multi-Tooth)
**What it is:** Uses adjacent teeth as anchors to replace one or more missing teeth.

**Pricing (3-unit bridge):**
- Cancun: $800 - $1,200
- US: $2,500 - $3,500
- Savings: 60-70%

**Timeline:** 10-14 days total
- Visit 1: Prepare anchor teeth, take impressions, temporary bridge
- Visit 2: Place permanent bridge (7 days later)

**Lifespan:** 5-10 years (typically)

---

## Aftercare Success Factors

**What leads to best outcomes:**
1. Follow all preoperative instructions (fasting, medications, etc.)
2. Take prescribed antibiotics fully even if feeling better
3. Take pain medication before pain becomes severe
4. Avoid smoking for at least 1 month (slows healing)
5. Avoid alcohol while taking pain meds or antibiotics
6. Soft diet for specified period (prevent damage to restoration)
7. Attend all follow-up appointments (critical for implants)
8. Use prescribed mouth rinse if provided
9. Maintain regular brushing and flossing
10. Schedule video checkups as recommended

**Typical Call Outcomes:**
- 98% of patients report excellent outcomes
- 95% would refer friends and family
- 87% say they'll return for additional work
- Only 2-3% request revisions (almost always minor)
